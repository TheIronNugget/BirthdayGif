Damned

Casted from the Sun

They sought refuge from the Queen of Shadows.

Where the veil is weak, they wait.

Though rare, faltering makes tears within the cloth.

Prisoners of thousands of years free? Do they seek peace? Maybe cooperation?

No, they seek you.

Your vessel.

But is even that enough?

I welcome you to the journey.

A walk of death, where we fight to understand and survive.
